Biorepository Services

The Weill biobank specializes in the meticulous acquisition and management of blood and CSF specimens. By adhering to the highest professional standards of ethical collection, storage, and distribution, we ensure that every sample retains its integrity and scientific value.

Data Services

Effective data management is just as critical as the physical samples themselves. Our Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is designed for comprehensive sample tracking from collection to distribution, as well as data annotation, and efficient workflow management to support advanced research.

Biobank Team

The Weill Institute for Neurosciences at UCSF is guided by a team of visionary leaders dedicated to advancing our understanding of the brain and nervous system. Through their expertise, commitment, and collaboration, they drive pioneering research and transformative patient care that redefine the boundaries of neuroscience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions? Explore our FAQ page to find answers to commonly asked questions about our services, research initiatives, and how to partner with us. Whether you're curious about sample access, data management, or compliance standards, our FAQ is here to help. Visit the page to learn more!
