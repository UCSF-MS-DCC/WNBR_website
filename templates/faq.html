WNBR Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access WNBR sample reports?

WNBR Primary Investigators and laboratory staff have access to reports. For other parties who need access, contact a WNBR Primary Investigator or the lab manager to discuss your needs and request access.

How can I reset my password?

To reset your password, contact a WNBR administrator using the form on the homepage of this website. Use the subject line: Reset Password.

What sample information is available to researchers?

Please use the form on the home page of this website to contact WNBR administrators to request a summary of data points available for samples.

Can samples be requested through this site?

Samples cannot be directly requested through this website. Please use the Sample Availability Request Form on the Biorepository page to contact our lab manager with your requirements and to discuss sample availability.
